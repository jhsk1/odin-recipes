Spaghetti Aglio e Olio

  • Spaghetti, 100g
  • 3 cloves of garlic
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Parmesan (optional)
  1. Bring water to boil and cook spaghetti al dente.
  2. Add chopped garlic and olive oil to pan, cooking on low heat until soft.
  3. Add cooked spaghetti to pan with small amount of pasta water.
  4. Cook until sauce is emulsified, adding salt to taste. Serve with parmesan if desired.
